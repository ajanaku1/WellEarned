# WellEarned Roadmap

## v1.0 — MONOLITH Hackathon (Current)
- AI meal analysis via camera (Gemini Vision)
- AI workout form coaching via camera (Gemini Video)
- Voice mood logging (Gemini Audio NLU)
- GPS workout location tagging + AI location insights
- Smart personalized push notifications
- Solana wall connection (Mobile Wallet Adapter)
- SKR streak reward system with multipliers
- On-chain daily claim transactions
- Cross-feature AI insights dashboard

## v1.1 — Post-Hackathon
- Social streaks: compete with friends on leaderboard
- Wearable integration: auto-log workouts from health sensors
- Expanded AI coaching: sleep tracking, hydration, macros

## v2.0 — On-Chain Health NFTs (Planned)
Milestone-based soulbound NFT badges minted to user wallets.
When a user hits major milestones (30-day streak, 100 meals logged, first AI insight unlocked),
the app mints a unique achievement NFT to their connected wallet via Metaplex.
These are permanent, transferable proof of health achievements living on Solana.
Implementation will use Underdog Protocol API for gas-efficient minting at scale.

## v2.1 — DAO Governance
- WellEarned DAO: SKR holders vote on reward rates and challenge types
- Community wellness challenges with pooled SKR prizes
