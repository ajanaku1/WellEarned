## Slide 3: The Solution

WellEarned combines three powerful forces:

1. **Multimodal AI Coaching** (Gemini) — Snap a meal, record a workout, voice your feelings
2. **Blockchain Rewards** (Solana) — Earn SKR tokens for daily health check-ins
3. **Streak Economics** — Consistency compounds: 7 days = 1.5x, 14 days = 2x, 30 days = 3x rewards

The result: an AI coach you can trust, with rewards you can spend.

## Slide 4: How It Works

**Daily Loop:**
1. Log a meal (photo) -> AI analyzes nutrition, scores 1-10, suggests healthier swaps
2. Log a workout (video) -> AI evaluates form, counts reps, assesses injury risk
3. Check in on mood (voice/journal) -> AI detects emotions, provides wellness tips

**When all 3 are done:**
- Claim daily SKR token reward on Solana
- On-chain memo records your streak (verifiable on Explorer)
- Streak multiplier increases your earnings
